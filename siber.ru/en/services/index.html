SIBER offers a wide range of services in the field of security, including armed physical security and counter-terrorism safety of the various facilities, design, modernization and maintenance of technical security equipment and fire protection systems, as well as ensuring safety of cargo in transit.

Companies within SIBER Group are operating in compliance with the law of the Russian Federation and related legislative acts on licensing of the certain types of activity.
