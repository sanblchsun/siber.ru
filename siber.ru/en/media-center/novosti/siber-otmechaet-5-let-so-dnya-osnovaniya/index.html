SIBER celebrates its 5th anniversary

 Holding company, which provides comprehensive security for Rostec enterprises, demonstrates stable growth

On April 19, 2018 JSC SIBER - holding company of the Rostec State Corporation - celebrates its fifth anniversary. Over a very short period of time, the holding company not only managed to solve an important task of forming a reliable security system at Rostec enterprises, but also combined a wide range of competencies necessary for successful competition in the Russian security services market.

The decision to create an integrated structure in the field of ensuring the security of organizations included in the management of the state corporation was adopted by the Board of the Rostec State Corporation in January 2013. The new structure, called "Security Technologies", was formed on the basis of the closed joint-stock company “RT-Guard”, which since 2010 provided departmental protection for Rostec enterprises.

Over the next two years, the company has made a real leap in development. In the holding there were new subsidiaries - departmental fire protection "RT-Fire Safety" and a network of private security organizations "RTO-Guard". Active development has received a direction of international cooperation: partners of the Russian holding are Chinese companies Huawei Security and DeWeGroup, as well as Singapore's AETOS (AETOS Holding Pte Ltd) and ST Electronics. Within the framework of the agreements signed, joint projects in the field of physical security, cargo escort, training and consulting are planned. In addition, as part of the meeting of the Committee for Strategic Planning under the Supervisory Board of the state corporation Rostec, chaired by the Minister of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation, D.V. Manturov there was made a decision on the need to include in the development strategy of "Security Technologies" the project to create, on the basis of the on-duty departmental guard unit, a fully functional Situation Center. The center will be located on the territory of the innovative complex "Rostec-City", which will be built on the site of the former Tushino airfield.

Expansion of competencies and the scope of services provided lead to the need to create a brand that would unite the companies that are parts of the holding. Such a brand became "SIBER". In autumn 2015, the new logo of the holding was officially introduced - a three-dimensional technological board, symbolizing complex protection from any threats.

2016 was marked by a series of significant events for the holding company. So, in April, one of the largest projects of the holding was launched - the construction of a multifunctional Training Center in the Smolensk region. Theoretical and practical training will be conducted at its base by the employees of “SIBER” and other Russian companies, as well as by foreign specialists in the field of security. In the process of training, it is planned to use special means and small arms fire weapons produced by the enterprises of the State Corporation “Rostec”.

An important stage in the history of “SIBER” was the creation of a joint Russian-Chinese enterprise "Hao Guard", which provides services to ensure the security of property and escort cargoes of Chinese companies on the territory of the Russian Federation. Partner from the Chinese side was the company Huawei Security.

International cooperation is one of the important activities of the holding. Thanks to successful cooperation with foreign companies, “SIBER” has the opportunity to use the experience and the latest developments of foreign colleagues. In addition, within the framework of strategic agreements, the Russian holding company can offer its customers a wide range of services abroad with the involvement of its foreign partners with the necessary infrastructure and competencies in the field of security. In recent years, leading international companies have become partners of “SIBER” - the Chinese security agency China Cityguard Security, the Indian company Checkmate, the South African BORA, the Austrian Association for Personal Protection and Security (BSA), and the French company "Amarante"(AMARANTE International).

Today, the structure of the “SIBER” Holding includes five organizations: the managing company JSC “SIBER”, the departmental protection of the State Corporation Rostek- “RT-Guard”, the departmental fire protection “RT-Fire Safety”, the network of private security organizations “RTO- Guard "and the joint Russian-Chinese enterprise " Hao Guard "LLC. Combining a wide range of competencies in the field of security, each of them successfully operates in its niche: physical security of stationary facilities, cargo protection during their transportation, fire safety, design, modernization, introduction and maintenance of engineering and security equipment and fire safety systems, private security activities.

Today, the departmental protection of Rostec is represented by 25 branches, providing security for more than 300 enterprises of the State Corporation located in Russia from Vladivostok to Kaliningrad.

The holding plans to expand its branch network in 2018, as well as to implement projects aimed to modernize security systems and increase the competitiveness of the services offered.
