Volobuyev Nikolay
Chairman of the Board of Directors of SIBER JSC
Born on February 24, 1952 in Rassvet, Mtsencky district of the Oryol Oblast

Education

In 1975 graduated from the Higher Red Banner School of the Committee of State Security of the USSR, with thw specialization in Law; PhD

Professional activity

1975 – 2004 – USSR State Security Committee, subsequently RF Federal Security Service;

2004 – 2006 – Deputy Head of the Federal Customs Service.

2006 – 2007 – Director for special assignments of the Federal State Unitary Enterprise Rosoboronexport

2007 – present – Deputy CEO of  Rostec

Honours

Rewarded with the Order of Military Merit, Order of Honor and a number of government medals.

Personal life

Mr Volobuev is married and has two daughters.
