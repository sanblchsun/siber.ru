The Head of SIBER’s Information Technology Service takes part in IT & Security Forum

﻿﻿Alexander Stolyarov, Head of Information technology service of SIBER, takes part in one of the largest IT events, which annually attracts a professional audience from all over Russia and Eastern Europe to IT - Security Forum 2019. The 13th ITSF is dedicated to the topic of digital transformation of enterprise operations. The speakers and guests of the forum will discuss issues related to cybersecurity, industry digitalization, as well as the latest legislative directives in the field of IT. The event runs from May 29 to May 31, 2019.
