Provision of a complete range of necessary security services to companies within the Rostec Corporation is the key task for SIBER.

Having optimized the property and cargo protection services for Rostec’ companies, SIBER is actively exploring and developing new business areas.

Following the Development Strategy 2020, approved by Rostec and aimed at reaching balance between long-term strategic interests of the State and the commercial goals, SIBER will focus on introducing innovative security products and services, as well as on establishing new joint ventures and reaching significant increase in number of customers.

History milestones
2010
Establishment of CJSC RT-Guard
April, 2010
  • On April 27, 2010 the Chairman of the Russian Federation Government Vladimir Putin signed Resolution No. 287 On approval of Regulations on in-house security of the State Corporation for Assistance to Development, Production and Export of Advanced Technology Industrial Products «Rostekhnologii»                
  • On July 12, 2010, according to Resolution No. 287 signed by the Chairman of the Russian Federation Government Vladimir Putin, Rostekhnologii State Corporation (since July 23, 2014 - Rostec) decided to establish RT-Guard CJSC in order to ensure in-house security of Rostec' companies
  • On July 21, 2010, CJSC RT-Guard was officially registered
2013
JSC Security Technologies was founded
April, 2013
  • On January 15, 2013, the Board of Rostec Corporation resolved to establish a holding company Security Technologies. JSC Security Technologies was formed on the basis of CJSC RT-Guard
  • On April 19, 2013, JSC Security Technologies was registered
2014
Establishment of JSC RT-Fire Protection
June, 2014
On June 25, 2014, JSC RT-Fire Protection was registered.
Agreements with Huawei Security and DeWeGroup, PRC
October, 2014
In October 2014, bilateral agreements between SIBER and two Chinese companies – Huawei Security and DeWeGroup – were signed.
2015
PSC RTO-GUARD Ltd. was established
March, 2015
On March 20, 2015, Private Security Company RTO-GUARD Ltd. was registered. RTO-Guard united a network of private security companies.
Agreement with AETOS (Singapore)
April, 2015
On April 15, 2015 SIBER signed strategic cooperation agreement with the subsidiary of the Temasek investment company – AETOS (AETOS Holding Pte Ltd).

A new logo of SIBER holding company was presented
September, 2015
In September 2015, a new logo of the holding company was presented – a three-dimensional technological shield symbolizing comprehensive protection from any threats. Visual style reflects the values declared by the company and broadcasts the image of SIBER as a high-technology Russian company offering best innovative security solutions.
Memorandum of Understanding with AETOS Holdings Pte Ltd
September, 2015

On September 22, 2015, SIBER signed a Memorandum of Understanding with AETOS Holdings Pte Ltd (Singapore)

Agreement with ST Electronics
November, 2015
In November 2015, JSC SIBER signed an agreement with ST Electronics, Singapore. According to the agreement, SIBER obtained an exclusive right to distribute the advanced solution by ST Electronics – the high-tech AgilFence perimeter intrusion detection system, the so-called "intellectual fence" – in Russia and in the countries of Eurasian Economic Union. SIBER has also obtained the license to complete assembly of the AgilFence cable in Russia under the brand name SIBER using the manufacturing facilities of Rostec Corporation
2016
Agreement with Checkmate, India
March, 2016
On March 28, 2016, was signed a partnership agreement aimed to ensure the safety of Russian companies in India and Indian companies in Russia. The Checkmate security company, which owns the Institute of Fire, Safety & Disaster Management (IFSDMS), acted as an Indian partner.
A resolution to set up a Training Center was made
April, 2016
On April 6, 2016, Board of Directors of JSC SIBER resolved to set up a Training Center.
Establishment of Hao Guard LLC
June, 2016
In June 2016, SIBER presented a joint Russian-Chinese security company Hao Guard that was set up to ensure the safety of Chinese companies in Russia.
Agreement with DeWeGroup, China
June, 2016
On June 25, 2016, SIBER signed another agreement with DeWeGroup (China) concerning the cooperation in the field of commercial security, safety of individuals and cargo in transit, as well as protection of infrastructure on the territory of EEU countries.
Agreement with BORA, South Africa
September, 2016
On September 15, 2016, a strategic partnership agreement was signed with BORA (security unit of the Anglo African Capital group).
2017
Agreement with BSA, Austria
April, 2017
On April 22, 2017, SIBER signed an agreement with the Bodyguard & Security Association (BSA). According this agreement, the parties established a strategic alliance in the field of security services, security consulting, risk assessment and risk mitigation, training and sales of technical safety and security equipment.
Agreement with Cityguard
October, 2017

SIBER Holding company signed a strategic partnership agreement with China Cityguard Security, the largest Chinese private security company.

The document defines the main areas of cooperation of the parties, including joint work on ensuring the security of international projects within the framework of the “One-Belt-One-Road” strategy. In addition, the document provides the cooperation on joint promotion of advanced Chinese technologies in the Russian market with the prospect of localizing production at Rostec's facilities.

Memorandum with AETOS Holding Pte Ltd (Singapore)
December, 2017
On December 13, 2017 SIBER has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with AETOS, within the framework of which the parties agreed to pool resources in the implementation of the project of the Situational Center of the State Corporation Rostec.
2018
Agreement with AMARANTE International (France)
April, 2018
On April 4, 2018 SIBER entered into a cooperation agreement with AMARANTE International (France). The agreement has been signed for purposes of forming a security alliance. The cooperation implies exchange of information, implementation of researches and commercial projects related to various services to be provided in Russia and other countries.
Memorandum with DeWeGroup and Far East Investment and Export Agency
April, 2018
SIBER, Chinese DeWe Group and Far East Investment and Export Agency, which is an autonomous non-profit organization, signed a memorandum on the construction of a shooting and training center in Vladivostok. They plan to develop the facility to become a tourist cluster in future.
Agreement on Implementation of the “Training and sports shooting complex” Investment Project
September, 2018
On September 12, 2018, SIBER, DeWe Group, Far East Investment and Export Agency and “Primorskoe Koltso” tourist and leisure cluster LLC signed an agreement on implementation of the investment project in the Far East region of the Russian Federation. The amount of investments will be around 10 000 000 USD (ten million US dollars).
2019
Memorandum of understanding on strategic cooperation with CETC (China)
August, 2019
On August 29, 2019, during the international aviation and space salon MAKS-2019, a Memorandum of understanding on strategic cooperation (in the field of fire monitoring equipment, fire protection equipment and unmanned aerial vehicles) between JSC SIBER and Chinese Corporation of electronic science and technology (China Electronics Technology Group Corporation, CETC) was signed.
Agreement on strategic cooperation with Falcon (Egypt)
November, 2019

On November 20, 2019 at the representative office of the Rostec State Corporation in Paris, a ceremony was held to sign an Agreement on strategic cooperation between SIBER and Egyptian company Falcon. The agreement defines the general principles of interaction between companies in the field of security, consulting, risk assessment and prevention. In the future, the parties plan to launch joint projects related to the provision of comprehensive security services in Russia and Egypt.

Strategic cooperation agreement with Middle East Security S.A.E. (Egypt)
November, 2019

On November 27, 2019 SIBER signed a strategic cooperation agreement with Egyptian company Middle East Security S. A. E.
