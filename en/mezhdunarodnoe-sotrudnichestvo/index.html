France
France
On April 04, 2018 SIBER holding company, a member of the Rostec State Corporation, and AMARANTE International, a French company, have entered into a strategic cooperation agreement.

The agreement has been signed for purposes of forming a security alliance. The cooperation implies exchange of information, implementation of researches and commercial projects related to various services to be provided in Russia and other countries.

Amarante possesses wide competences in security systems planning at large industrial and state-owned facilities. Their clients include governmental and commercial entities, including one of the largest museums of the world, the Louvre, the CHANEL company and the Guiana Space Centre (Kourou Cosmodrome) jointly operated by the European Space Agency and the National Centre for Space Studies (France).

Egypt
Egypt

On November 20, 2019 at the representative office of the Rostec State Corporation in Paris, a ceremony was held to sign an Agreement on strategic cooperation between SIBER and Egyptian company Falcon. The agreement defines the general principles of interaction between companies in the field of security, consulting, risk assessment and prevention. In the future, the parties plan to launch joint projects related to the provision of comprehensive security services in Russia and Egypt.

On November 27, 2019 SIBER signed a strategic cooperation agreement with the Egyptian company Middle East Security S. A. E.

Austria
Austria

On April 22, 2017, Sochi hosted the signing of an Agreement on strategic cooperation between the holding company “SIBER” providing security of State Corporation Rostech enterprises and Austrian Bodyguard & Security Association, BSA. Under the terms of the concluded agreement, the sides intend to create a strategic alliance the purpose of which is to cooperate in the areas of security, consulting, assessment and prevention of risks as well as training and implementation of technical means.

BSA Company, based in Innsbruck (Austria) and operating in Western Europe, has an extensive experience in the field of security services. The basic activity – is security, provision of detective services, as well as training services. In addition, the Bodyguard & Security Association carries out trade in technical means related to security.

The conclusion of the strategic partnership agreement is primarily aimed at providing security services to State Corporation Rostech enterprises in the BSA countries - the countries of Eastern and Western Europe. The main volume of services is supposed to be provided with cargo escort and property protection during international exhibitions and other presentation international events and forums in which enterprises included in State Corporation Rostech take part.

The holding company “SIBER” offers comprehensive security services to existing and potential customers of BSA on the territory of the Russian Federation in return. Cooperation with BSA allows to expand the geography of the presence of the holding Company "SIBER" in the provision of its services, using the infrastructure of the Austrian company. The parties also intend to cooperate in the provision of advisory services in the field of security, in particular when checking the integrity of counterparties - in preparation for making the deals involving business entities of the Russian Federation and countries of Western and Eastern Europe.

A separate important block on which the parties intend to focus their attention is the development and implementation of joint training programs for specialists in the field of security and safety. BSA Company has a considerable experience and valuable developments in the field of security of cargo escort by sea, as well as the programs to counter piracy attacks and complex solutions for ensuring the security of facilities. SIBER, while providing in-house security services for the Rostec State Corporation companies, has a significant experience in protection of strategic facilities and escorting special cargoes.

Republic of Singapore
Republic of Singapore

On April 15, 2015 SIBER signed strategic cooperation agreement with AETOS (AETOS Holding Pte Ltd).

On September 22, 2015, SIBER signed a Memorandum of Understanding with AETOS Holdings Pte Ltd (Singapore). The document regulates the cooperation of two companies in the field of perimeter security, integrated site control systems, creation of situational site control centres, intelligent control systems for the transportation of dangerous and secret cargoes and in other spheres.

On December 13, 2017 SIBER signed the Memorandum of Understanding aimed at development of the Rostec State Corporation Command Center.

On December 13, 2017 SIBER has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with AETOS, within the framework of which the parties agreed to pool resources in the implementation of the project of the Situational Center of the Rostec State Corporation.


People's Republic of China
People's Republic of China

In October 2014 the bilateral agreements were signed with the Chinese companies Huawei Security and DeWeGroup. In prospect — the creation of the joint projects in the sphere of physical security, escort of cargoes, training and consulting.

On June 25, 2016 the Russian holding company signed an agreement with Chinese DeWeGroup on cooperation in the sphere of business protection, safeguarding of people and transported cargoes, as well as infrastructure, located on the territory of the EAEU countries.

In June 2016 SIBER presented a joint Russian-Chinese security company Hao Guard that was set up to ensure the safety of Chinese companies in Russia.

In October 2017 SIBER signed a strategic partnership agreement with China Cityguard Security, the largest Chinese private security company. The document defines the main areas of cooperation of the parties, including joint work on ensuring the security of international projects within the framework of the “One-Belt-One-Road” strategy. In addition, the document provides the cooperation on joint promotion of advanced Chinese technologies in the Russian market with the prospect of localizing production at Rostec's facilities.

In April 2018 SIBER, Chinese DeWe Group and Far East Investment and Export Agency, which is an autonomous non-profit organization, signed a memorandum on the construction of a shooting and training center in Vladivostok. They plan to develop the facility to become a tourist cluster in the future.

Republic of India
Republic of India

On March 28, 2016 the agreement of cooperation in the field of security protection of the Russian enterprises in India and Indian companies in the Russian Federation was signed. The partner on Indian part was Checkmate security company, owning the IFSDMS emergency management and fire safety training facility. The Indian representatives came up with approach to establish a joint venture on SIBER and Checkmate basis.

Republic of South Africa
Republic of South Africa
On September 15, 2016 the agreement of strategic cooperation with BORA, security detachment of the Anglo African Capital group, was signed. BORA (Business Opportunity and Risk Assessment) is a security company established in 2007, rendering the security services in a wide range. It is in successful operation on the market of South Africa and adjacent states, it has own training facility, the largest one in the RSA. The signed document affirms the intentions of SIBER and BORA to establish the long-term mutually beneficial partnership, and it also determines the principles and procedure of cooperation between the companies. Concurrently some areas of cooperation are worked over, including: joint promotion of the products manufactured by the Rostec enterprises on the RSA market, coordination in the line of the Russian-South African Business Council and realization of the joint project of protection and security personnel training.
